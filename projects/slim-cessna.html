Slim Cessna’s Auto Club at Downtown Artery

“This is how it’s always been… This is how we do things in the country!”

I got the opportunity to create a poster for Slim Cessna’s show at the Downtown Artery in Fort Collins. Being a fan of their live shows it was a fun project to work on.

Artwork for the band tends to be dark southern gothic, so we experimented with a brighter color pallete while still holding onto some of those darker overtones in the illustration.

I worked with Ink Lounge in Denver to screenprint the posters.
